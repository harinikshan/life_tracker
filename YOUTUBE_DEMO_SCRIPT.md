# LifeTracker YouTube Demo & Script

Creating a compelling demo video is key to grabbing attention on platforms like YouTube Shorts or TikTok. Below is a structured script tailored for a 45-60 second engaging video demo.

### 🎬 Video Setup
- **Format:** Vertical (9:16) for YouTube Shorts/TikTok, or Horizontal (16:9) for standard YouTube.
- **Vibe:** Minimalist, productive, slight urgency. Use a calm but serious electronic/lo-fi track in the background.

---

### 📜 The Script

**[0:00 - 0:05] The Hook**  
*Visual:* You scrolling endlessly on YouTube or a busy Reddit feed.  
*Voiceover:* "Are you wasting hours online without even realizing it? Here’s a minimal Chrome Extension that completely fixed my scrolling addiction."

**[0:05 - 0:15] The Setup**  
*Visual:* Screen recording of clicking the LifeTracker extension icon. The sleek popup appears. You input your birth date (e.g., 1995) and lifespan (e.g., 80) and click save.  
*Voiceover:* "It’s called LifeTracker. You just put in your birth date and estimated lifespan..."

**[0:15 - 0:25] The Reveal**  
*Visual:* The popup transitions into the "See your time" view. The screen zooms in closely on the beautiful PM-grade blue pie charts filling up.  
*Voiceover:* "...and it instantly calculates exactly how much of your life you have already spent. It breaks time down into 7 metrics: your total Life, the Year, the Month, Week, Day, Hour, and Minute."

**[0:25 - 0:40] The Action (The Widget)**  
*Visual:* Transition back to the YouTube homepage. The elegant dark-glass widget is sitting in the top right corner. The mouse hovers over the different dots: `YR`, `DAY`, and `MIN`. The hyper-legible tooltip pops up reading "x days left", "x mins left". The `MIN` dot pulses.  
*Voiceover:* "When you browse the web, this completely unobtrusive widget floats in the corner. If you hover over the dots, you see exactly how much time is left in your day and your current hour. Look closely, and you can literally see the 'Minute' circle ticking away in real-time."

**[0:40 - 0:50] Call to Action (Outro)**  
*Visual:* The extension's logo or Chrome Webstore install page.  
*Voiceover:* "It's the ultimate reality check for your daily productivity. Stop wasting time—go download LifeTracker today. Link in the description!"

---

### 🎥 Filming Tips
1. **Screen Record:** Use a clean browser without a cluttered bookmark bar.
2. **Zoom In:** When showing the tooltip hover, scale up the video (punch in) in your editing software so viewers on mobile can clearly read the crisp blue UI.
3. **Pacing:** Keep the cuts fast. Don't linger on typing in your birthday for more than 2 seconds.
